About Me

Hi, I'm Evangelina Morris —
emerging HR professional & curious learner.

I'm at the beginning of my HR journey, and this blog is my way of logging my learning. I'm not an expert, I'm someone who's genuinely curious about people, workplaces, and how organisations can do better by the humans inside them.

I started this space because I couldn't find many voices talking about what it's actually like to break into HR — the worry, the study, the questions you feel embarrassed to ask. So I'm writing that blog myself, for anyone else who's in the same boat.

When I'm not studying or combing through LinkedIn at 11pm, you'll find me walking with my friends, cross stitching, or deep in a novel I should have put down hours ago.
